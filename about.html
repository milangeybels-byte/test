About Stockfile

Curated, concise market reasoning

Stockfile is a curated market-analysis tool that translates important global developments into potential near-term market implications. We focus on signal, not noise — selecting only developments judged material enough to matter for markets over the coming weeks and months.

How Stockfile works

Each entry is the result of a structured reasoning process:

event → implied catalyst → exposed business models → key assumptions

Outputs are concise and opinionated; assumptions are stated explicitly so readers can evaluate the thesis.

Direction & confidence

Up / Down indicate potential near-term pressure (not price targets). Confidence expresses analytical conviction (High / Medium / Low).

What Stockfile is — and is not

  • Stockfile is a thinking aid for market-aware readers.
  • Stockfile is not investment advice nor a recommendation to buy or sell securities.
